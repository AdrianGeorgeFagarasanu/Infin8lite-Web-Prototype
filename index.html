M8TRIX OLED Stage Lighting WINS LDI Excellence Award in Las Vegas


Live Design Int’l has been the leading tradeshow and conference for live design professionals from all around the globe.
M8TRIX programmable, inter-active stage lighting solves many age-old health and safety problems that have plagued lighting technicians, stage crews and entertainers for years. Improving Comfort, Concentration, Focus, Eye Health, Safety, Programming, Efficiency, Storage, shipping and assembly issues. Heat, glare, UV rays and flickering found in traditional lighting are eliminated with OLED lighting. M8TRIX is a modular, extendable system created with individual 18.6" square panels for simple, fast set-up. Each panel has 64 LG Display OLED lights with a built-in controller. A combination of several M8TRIX arrays can be arranged to create walls, pillars and ceiling light to display infinite combinations of lighting SFX, animated text and interactive video. The M8TRIX comes complete with the options of DMX or ARTNET including powercon and safety cable for easy set-up and instant display. In addition, M8TRIX has custom OLED panels with digital control to give a range of precise warm white dimming.
